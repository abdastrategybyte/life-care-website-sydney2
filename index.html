NDIS SUPPORT

Navigating Your NDIS Journey

As a registered NDIS provider, we help participants access and maximize their NDIS plans to achieve their goals for independence and quality of life.

  1. Ensure your NDIS plan includes funding for the relevant supports
  2. Contact us to discuss your needs and service availability
  3. We'll help complete required service agreements
  4. Schedule your first appointment at our clinic or your home

Our services may be funded under these NDIS support categories:

  • Improved Daily Living (Therapy Supports)
  • Improved Health and Wellbeing
  • Home Modifications
  • Assistive Technology

We work with all plan management options:

  • NDIS-managed funding
  • Plan-managed funding
  • Self-managed funding

NDIS Frequently Asked Questions

The NDIS may fund home modifications that help you:

  • Access and move around your home safely
  • Use bathroom and kitchen independently
  • Enter and exit your home
  • Live more independently

Our services are available to:

  • NDIS participants with funding
  • Private clients seeking our services
  • Individuals referred by health professionals

Referrals can come from:

  • Your NDIS planner or coordinator
  • General practitioners
  • Other therapists
  • Self-referral
